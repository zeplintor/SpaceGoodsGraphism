About SpaceGoods

Bringing cosmic chill to Earth, one planet at a time. We're not just a brand, we're a vibe.

Our Story

SpaceGoods was born from a simple idea: the universe is vast, life is short, and everyone deserves to chill. We create premium edibles and merch that embody the slow, peaceful energy of floating through space.

Our iconic sleepy planet character represents the universal need to slow down, breathe, and enjoy the cosmic journey.

Hand-Drawn Everything

Every illustration, every button, every design element is hand-drawn with love. We reject digital perfection in favor of organic, imperfect, human creativity.

Our black and white aesthetic with halftone textures pays homage to zines, underground comics, and print culture.
