Frequently Asked Questions

Where do your events take place?

Our events take place in carefully selected venues for their acoustics and unique atmosphere. Exact addresses are revealed to ticket holders a few days before the event to maintain the intimacy of our gatherings.

What's the dress code?

Come as you are! SpaceGoods Music celebrates personal expression. Many attendees love wearing cosmic, psychedelic, or simply comfortable outfits. The essential thing is to feel good.

Are events outdoor or indoor?

We organize both! Our summer events often take place outdoors (forests, rooftops, gardens), while our winter events favor warm indoor spaces with immersive audiovisual installations.

Can I bring my own SpaceGoods?

Our events offer a selection of SpaceGoods edibles available for purchase on-site. For safety and compliance reasons, we ask that you do not bring external products.
